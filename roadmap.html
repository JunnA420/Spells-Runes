Air Magic

A New Lost Echo

The first element — Air — awakens. Rooted in agility and force, Air magic grants its wielder mastery over movement, pushing foes aside and bending the wind to their will. Alongside it, the foundations of Flux are laid.

  • Flux resource & alignment system
  • Air spells up to Tier II
  • Sylphweed — found atop mountain peaks
  • Spell hotbar, radial menu & cast timing

Water Magic

The Second Echo

Still and deep, Water magic flows between offense and restoration. It heals the wounded, erodes resistance and turns the tide of battle through patience and precision.

  • Healing Sprinkle — area restore
  • Water Spray — utility & knockback
  • Deep Healing — sustained ritual
  • Deep Sea Pearl — harvested from the deep ocean floor

Fire Magic

The Third Echo

Volatile and relentless, Fire magic burns through defenses and sustains itself through aggression. The fiercer the battle, the stronger the flame.

  • Spark — fire cone burst
  • Cook in Hand — utility
  • Hot Skin — passive heat aura
  • Ignis Crystal — near volcanic hotspots & equatorial depths

Earth Magic

The Fourth Echo

Immovable and enduring, Earth magic is the art of outlasting your enemy. Fortify your body, reshape terrain and let the land itself become your weapon.

  • Stone Skin — defense buff
  • Earth Wall — terrain barrier
  • Earth Clone — decoy entity
  • Rhea's Crown — a rare mushroom in temperate forests

Deeper Mastery

The four base Echoes grow deeper. Tier III and IV spells are unlocked for Air, Water, Fire and Earth — demanding greater Flux mastery and offering substantially more power.

  • Tier III & IV spells for all 4 elements
  • Advanced Flux alignment mechanics
  • New activator rituals per element
  • Spell combo system — early access

Runes

The Written Word

Magic does not have to be cast — it can be written. Runes are inscribed symbols that bind spells into objects, surfaces and rituals, allowing magic to persist long after the mage has left.

  • Rune inscription on items & blocks
  • Spell binding — delayed & triggered effects
  • Ritual circles — area-based casting
  • Combinable rune sequences

Lightning Magic

A Derived Echo

Born from the union of Air and Fire, Lightning is raw speed made lethal. It strikes before enemies can react and chains between targets with terrifying efficiency.

  • Requires Air + Fire alignment
  • Chain lightning — multi-target
  • Static field — area denial
  • Lightning Echo activator

Ice Magic

A Derived Echo

Born from Water and Air, Ice magic is control made manifest. Freeze enemies in place, shatter them with concussive force and reshape the battlefield with barriers of solid cold.

  • Requires Water + Air alignment
  • Frost Lance — piercing projectile
  • Glacial Shell — ice armor
  • Ice Echo activator

Nature Magic

A Derived Echo

Born from Earth and Water, Nature magic is the patient force of growth given purpose. It heals, entangles and summons the living world as an extension of the mage's will.

  • Requires Earth + Water alignment
  • Entangle — root & slow
  • Bloom — area regeneration
  • Nature Echo activator

The Upper Limit
