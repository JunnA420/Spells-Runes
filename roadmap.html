A New Flux Echo

The first element awakens. Air magic grants mastery over movement, pushing foes aside and bending the wind to your will. The foundations of Flux are laid here.

  • Flux Echo: Sylphweed — atop mountain peaks
  • ~10–15 spells up to Tier II
  • Flux resource & alignment system
  • Spell hotbar, radial menu & cast timing

Runes

The Written Word

Magic does not have to be cast — it can be written. Runes are inscribed symbols that bind spells into objects, surfaces and rituals, allowing magic to persist long after the mage has left.

  • Rune inscription on items & blocks
  • Spell binding — delayed & triggered effects
  • Ritual circles — area-based casting
  • Combinable rune sequences

A Derived Flux Echo

Born from the union of Air and Fire, Lightning is raw speed made lethal. It strikes before enemies can react and chains between targets with terrifying efficiency.

  • Flux Echo: requires Air + Fire alignment
  • ~10–15 spells up to Tier II
  • Chain lightning — multi-target
  • Static field — area denial

  • Flux Echo: requires Water + Air alignment
  • ~10–15 spells up to Tier II
  • Frost Lance — piercing projectile

Nature Magic

A Derived Flux Echo

Born from Earth and Water, Nature magic is the patient force of growth given purpose. It heals, entangles and summons the living world as an extension of the mage's will.

  • Flux Echo: requires Earth + Water alignment

Tier Expansion II

The Upper Limit

Tier V and beyond — the rarest and most demanding spells in the game. Few mages will reach this level. Fewer still will survive long enough to use them.

  • Ultimate abilities with long cast times

  • Mastery titles & visual effects
