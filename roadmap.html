A New Flux Echo

Fast, aggressive and unrelenting. Air magic rewards those who never stop moving — outmaneuver enemies, strike hard and vanish before the counterattack arrives.

  • Flux Echo: Sylphweed — atop mountain peaks
  • ~10–15 spells up to Tier II
  • Flux resource & alignment system
  • Spell hotbar, radial menu & cast timing

Deeper Mastery

The four base Echoes grow deeper. Tier III and IV spells are unlocked — demanding greater Flux mastery and offering substantially more power.

Planned

Runes

The Body as Canvas

Runes are symbols of power inscribed upon the mage's own body. Each rune awakens a passive force attuned to an element — amplifying spells, granting resistances and bending natural laws in your favor.

  • Runes inscribed on the player character
  • Passive buffs that complement element alignment
  • Combinable rune sequences for synergy effects
  • Unlock deeper runes via Flux mastery
